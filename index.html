January VOC Report

The year 2025 started positively, with an NPS of 30 and a CSAT score of 73%. However it is important to highlight that the Bank’s CSAT performance continued to fall short of its target (85%). Since January 2023, CSAT scores have consistently remained below the 80% mark, with the last peak of 82% recorded in January 2023. This underscores the ongoing need to prioritize and implement targeted strategies to improve customer satisfaction.

03

March VOC/Digital Product Report

The bank’s NPS climbed by 13 points to 36 in March, rebounding from a 7-point dip in February and reaching its highest level since October 2022. This was driven by a rise in promoters across most products. Customer Satisfaction also rose by 6% from February; however, it has remained below the 80% mark since January 2023, when it last recorded 82%.

Bankwide NPS : 36
CSAT : 76%
